Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how I-llumin-A ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our automated social media content publishing application.

1. Who We Are

I-llumin-A is an AI-powered automated content creation and publishing service operating on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. The application publishes AI-generated short-form educational videos about artificial intelligence and cultural curiosities.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects minimal information. We only collect the email address of the account owner/operator for administrative and contact purposes. We do not collect personal data from viewers or followers.

  • We do not collect names, phone numbers, or postal addresses from users.
  • We do not use GPS, access contact lists, or use device cameras.
  • We do not use advertising tracking or remarketing services.
  • We do not use Google Analytics or similar third-party analytics tools.

3. How We Use Information

The email address collected is used solely to:

  • Communicate regarding the operation of the service.
  • Respond to inquiries about our content.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party platforms to publish content:

  • TikTok — Content Posting API (subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy)
  • Meta / Instagram — Graph API (subject to Meta's Privacy Policy)
  • YouTube / Google — YouTube Data API v3 (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

We use these APIs solely to publish video content. We do not extract or store any user data from these platforms.

5. Data Retention

We retain operational logs for a maximum of 30 days. No personal data of viewers or followers is stored.

6. GDPR — European Users

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data.
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • The right to restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object to processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
